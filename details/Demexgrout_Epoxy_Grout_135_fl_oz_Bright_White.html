  Demexgrout Epoxy Grout is a high-performance solution designed for tile and stone applications. Its waterproof and stain-resistant properties make it perfect for bathrooms, kitchens, and other moisture-prone areas. With a non-porous structure, it ensures better hygiene and longevity, while its resistance to chemicals, mildew, and mold offers unmatched durability. The ready-to-use formulation simplifies the installation process, allowing for quick application and clean-up without the need for additional sealing or mixing. Its bright white color maintains consistency over time, unlike traditional cement grouts that may darken with age or exposure. This product is ideal for both residential and commercial spaces, providing a reliable, long-lasting finish.
  This epoxy grout is an excellent choice for floors and walls, as it can endure harsh cleaning agents and maintain its appearance for years. Its flexibility allows for minor movement in the tiled surface, preventing cracks in environments where structural changes are possible. The wide temperature tolerance ensures it can be used in both indoor and outdoor settings, even in freezing conditions. For best results, use a dual barrel caulking gun for application, and remember that it is not water cleanable. Simply scrape off excess residue after the grout cures in about four hours at 75 °F.
