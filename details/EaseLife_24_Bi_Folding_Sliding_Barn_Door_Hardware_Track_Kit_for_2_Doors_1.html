EaseLife 24" Bi-Folding Sliding Barn Door Hardware Track Kit

Transform your space with the ultimate in sliding door hardware. This track kit is engineered for smooth, quiet operation and long-lasting performance. Whether you're looking to create an open yet private area in your home or office, this solution delivers both style and functionality.

Crafted from heavy-duty solid steel, the EaseLife hardware ensures durability and strength for years of use. The fine black powder-coated finish not only enhances the appearance but also offers excellent anti-corrosion protection, keeping your doors looking pristine without maintenance.

Features ball bearings for effortless sliding, having been tested to over 100,000 rolls for reliability and quiet operation. The kit includes all essential parts: a 28" rail, two rollers, two roller rotating bases, a roller fixator, three rail supports, three hinges, a floor guide, and a sealing strip. All components are precision-engineered to fit up to two doors with a thickness range of 1-3/8" to 1-3/4", ensuring a perfect match for your space.

This bi-folding door hardware is designed to provide a seamless installation experience, making it ideal for DIY enthusiasts and professional installers alike. It offers a minimalist look that blends effortlessly with your interior design, while also reducing drafts and noise with the built-in sealing strip.

Choose the EaseLife barn door track kit for a reliable, easy install, and stylish solution. It's the perfect choice for those who want to enjoy the benefits of sliding doors without compromising on quality or quietness.
