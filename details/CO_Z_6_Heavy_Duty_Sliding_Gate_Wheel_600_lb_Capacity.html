Reliable Sliding Gate Wheel for Metal Structures

Featuring a heavy-duty zinc-plated bracket, these gate wheels are engineered to resist corrosion and maintain strength even in the harshest weather conditions. The solid construction ensures minimal wear and tear, providing years of reliable service.

The dual wheel design enhances weight distribution and stability, reducing resistance and improving the overall efficiency of your gate movement. This makes it ideal for structures that require consistent support without sagging or dragging.

CO-Z gate wheels include all necessary hardware, making installation quick and convenient. The adjustable U-shaped bolt fits round tubes ranging from 1-5/8" to 2", offering a versatile solution for various gate types.
