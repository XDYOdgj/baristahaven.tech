Introducing the Hornbill Smart Lock WiFi Deadbolt

Upgrade your home security with this advanced 10-in-1 smart lock designed for modern living and rental properties. It brings convenience and safety together with innovative features that make traditional keys a thing of the past.

Smart Security at Your Fingertips

The Hornbill Smart Lock WiFi Deadbolt features a built-in biometric fingerprint reader, enabling effortless and secure access for you and your family. It also includes a Bluetooth keypad and virtual passcode system, so you can manage access for multiple users with ease.

Smart Home Integration Made Simple

With seamless compatibility with Alexa and Google Home, you can control your lock using voice commands, making your smart home setup even more intuitive. The device also supports remote locking and unlocking via the Smart Life app, all while providing real-time door logs and alerts.

Enjoy added convenience with the automatic lock timer and passage mode, which allow you to set timed locks or temporary access for guests. This makes the Hornbill Smart Lock ideal for residential and commercial settings, ensuring you're always in control.
