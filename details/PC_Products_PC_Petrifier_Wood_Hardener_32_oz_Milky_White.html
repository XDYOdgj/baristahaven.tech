PC Products PC-Petrifier - Water-Based Wood Hardener

When wood is damaged by insects or rot, it can weaken significantly over time. PC-Petrifier is a powerful water-based wood hardener designed to restore strength and stability to such materials. This innovative product penetrates deeply into the wood fibers, reinforcing them from within to provide long-lasting durability. It's perfect for woodworkers, DIYers, and homeowners looking to repair, enhance, or extend the life of their wooden projects without the need for replacement.

Key Features

  • Repairs insect or rot damage: PC-Petrifier effectively tackles damage caused by termites, carpenter ants, and decay, making your wood stronger and more resilient.
  • Water-based formula: Safe, non-toxic, and environmentally friendly, this wood hardener is easy to apply and dries quickly, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor use.
  • No mixing required: Use it straight from the container for simple, efficient application, saving time and effort during restoration.
  • Prepares for filling and painting: After application, the wood is ready for wood filler, sanding, priming, and painting to achieve a smooth, professional finish.
  • Improves hardness and durability: Reinforce your wood with PC-Petrifier to increase its strength and extend the lifespan of your wooden project.

Why Choose PC-Petrifier?

Whether you're restoring an antique table, repairing weathered wooden decks, or fixing cracked wood panels, PC-Petrifier is the trusted solution for wood restoration. It can be brushed, sprayed, or injected directly into the wood pores, offering flexibility and ease of use. This product not only repairs the damage but also improves the wood's overall condition, giving you the ability to save your pieces instead of replacing them. By increasing the hardness of the wood, PC-Petrifier helps maintain the original appearance and feel of your project, making it a valuable tool for anyone working with damaged wood.
